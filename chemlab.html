Laboratory work is my FAVORITE part of chemistry, carrying out experiment myself is a magical process. Instead of formula or functions to recite, I prefer to remember those theories with practice test. Though the space is limited for me to do the trials, I tried my best to fulfill my what I would like to pursue in my Chemistry study.
All in all, CHEM IS TRY!
I borrowed my mum’s stove to heat “Toilet Bowl Sanitizer” which contains HCl. What I wanted to test is whether the hydrochloric acid gas would spread out during the process of heating. Hydrochloric acid gas is corrosive so I had to use kitchen ventilator as a substitute of fume cupboard. The result of test was obvious, the chloride ion and the copper on the stove top reacted then successfully derived from the flame color experiment that there was hydrochloric acid gas spillage. (the copper ion flame color was green)
A failed experiment. LOL
I was expected to make a zinc and copper chemistry battery, with too small resistor (conduct wire) the electric current was large in the instant, which caused the whole system to be on fire.
I increased the resistance of the whole system, “Eelectrolytic Copper Refining”. Since a long period of time is needed to wait for crystallization, I added some oil to insulate the solution from air to prevent evaporation. The time of waiting was torturing, but the product showed was worthy.
That’s my mystery recipe of making Slime!!!!!!!! A spoon of glue and a few drops of contact lens washing solution mixed together, then, STIRRRRRRRRRR! Hand sanitizer will help to form dense bubbles!!!!!
