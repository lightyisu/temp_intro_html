The process of making pendants from a raw amber stone is amazing !!!!! Repeated polishing with sandpaper in different thicknesses of particles is exhausting and boring. Please be patient!!!!! Over-rated speed of grinding will leave scratch on the amber that ruins all the efforts. One of the most difficult to make is my horoscope symbol (Capricornus), its long thin “tail” is so vulnerable that extra attention should be attached.
